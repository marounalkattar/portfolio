CAR Robot

Car robot project

The car robot is a project based on an Arduino uno controlling a car of two or for motors. It has many stages detailed down below. A YouTube video will be on the top of this text and the link of GitHub code will be: -------

Phase1: timing control robot

The purpose of this phase is to make sure that my robot component is good and that the language is well written. In this phase the robot will go forward 2 second, backward 2 second, to the left 2 second, right 2 second and finally stop 2 second.

Phase2: PC communication

In this phase the robot car will take order from the Serial monitor so when I press f or F the car will go forward, S or s the car will stop, when pressing B or b the car will go backward, l or L the car will move to the left and R or r the car will go to the right.

Phase3: Smartphone control

I have made an android application to control the robot. See the screenshot of the application interface. The smartphone and the robot are connected via Bluetooth.

Phase4: Accelerometer control

The robot will be controlled by the accelerometer sensor of my smartphone. This sensor is an X, Y and Z sensor and will measure the orientation of my phone by giving feedback to the Arduino to move the car.

Phase5: Voice control

I created an application to manipulate my robot over voice control system so when I say “go forward” the robot must go forward.

Phase6: Black and White line follower

In this stage the robot will follow colored line. First, the line will be black and then it will be white.

Phase7: Object follower

The robot car will follow an object as the instructions.

Phase8: Object Avoid

An ultra-sonic sensor will control the robot, so the car won’t smash up with obstacles.

Phase9: light follower

As it is said, the robot will follow the light
